She is a member of the Stellaron Hunters who is calm, collected, and beautiful. Her record on the wanted list of the Interastral Peace Corporation only lists her name and her hobby. People have always imagined her to be elegant, respectable, and in pursuit of things of beauty... even in combat.

She, along with Silver Wolf, are the playable characters in the game's prologue.
